What is driverless cars/autonomous cars/self-driving cars? How does it work?

Driverless cars or autonomous cars or however you want to call it are vehicles capable of sensing its environment and operating without human involvement.

How does it work? These types of vehicles rely on sensors, actuators, complex algorithms, machine systems, and powerful processors. Our products are at a level 4 of Vehicle Autonomy, according to the 6 levels of Vehicle Autonomy.

What is the benefits and drawbacks of autonomous vehicles?

How do we address our products through ethical means?

People want to see more improvements and increased safety before seeing AVTs on the road or for widespread use. Many felt that they would be more inclined to ride an AV in a smaller setting, such as a parking lot.

We are looking for ways to make this type of technology more affordable for the average joe. We don't want this technology to be just for the elite, we want it for all. By providing the product on larger scales to companies instead of the average consumer, we could achieve this.

The future will see robots and technology change our daily life but it will not replace them. Our products still require human intellect, compassion, and creativity.
